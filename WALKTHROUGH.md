# RentShield - User Guide

Welcome to RentShield, an interactive tool helping NC tenants understand their rights and generate legal demand letters.

## Overview

RentShield allows you to:
1.  **Assess Your Situation**: Answer simple questions about your housing issue.
2.  **Understand Your Rights**: Learn about specific NC statutes (e.g., repairs, security deposits).
3.  **Take Action**: Generate a professional letter to send to your landlord or a summary to take to a lawyer.

## How to Use

### 1. Select a Scenario
On the Home page, choose the issue that matches your situation:
- **Repairs & Maintenance**: If you have mold, broken HVAC, or plumbing issues.
- **Security Deposit**: If you moved out >30 days ago and haven't received your deposit.
- **Eviction (Non-Payment)**: If you received a 10-Day Notice or Court Papers.

### 2. Answer Questions
Follow the step-by-step questions. 
- Use the **Back** button if you need to change an answer.
- The **Progress Bar** shows how far along you are.

### 3. Review Your Result
Upon finishing, you will see a Result Page with:
- **Outcome Summary**: e.g., "Landlord Obligation Triggered".
- **Legal Insight**: A brief explanation of the law.
- **Generated Letter**: A pre-written letter customized with your details.

### 4. Special Features

#### Advocate View (New!)
If you are working with a housing counselor or lawyer:
1.  On the Result Page, click the **"Advocate View"** toggle button.
2.  The view changes from a letter to a **Case Facts Summary**.
3.  Print or Copy this summary to give to your advocate. This saves time during intake!

#### Resources
Click "View Tenant Resources" in the footer to find phone numbers for Legal Aid of NC, NC 211, and other support services.

## For Community Partners (Kiosk Setup)
RentShield is designed to run on tablets in help centers.
- **Privacy**: No user data is saved after the session ends (unless they copy/print).
- **Reset**: Simply click "Start Over" or refresh the page to clear all data for the next user.

## Printing
Click the **"Print Letter"** button to open a printer-friendly version. This version removes all menus/buttons and formats the text professionally.
